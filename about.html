About CTX-EOR

Hiring has changed. We're here to help you change with it — without guesswork, friction, or risk.

Built for the Future of Work

CTX-EOR was founded to help U.S.-based companies hire smarter. We believe in trial-based hiring, structured evaluations, and compliant payroll infrastructure — because the future demands more than temp help promises and global complexity. We stay grounded:

  • U.S. compliance
  • North American employers
  • Real-world hiring results

We're not a temp agency. We're not a shadow staffing firm. We're your Employer of Record, your payroll platform, and your hiring operations system — rolled into one.

Who We Serve

  • Employers who want to test before they commit
  • Founders scaling lean teams without HR red tape
  • Ops leaders managing compliance-sensitive hires
  • Managers hiring for fit, not just qualifications
  • Companies experimenting with fractional, phased, or project-based work

From startup to scale-up — we help you hire with structure, insight, and flexibility.

We Handle the Risk — You Focus on Fit

When you use CTX-EOR, candidates are employed by us during their evaluation period. We manage:

  • Legal employment status
  • Payroll, W-2, and tax filing
  • Federal and state compliance
  • I-9 document verification
  • Benefits and insurance
  • Onboarding and exit workflows

You stay focused on one thing: Is this the right person for your team?

Why “CTX”?

CTX stands for Context — because that’s what hiring decisions should be made in. We don’t just provide resumes or screening scores. We give you structured performance insights in your real-world setting.

Because hiring is about what works at your company, not what works in theory.
